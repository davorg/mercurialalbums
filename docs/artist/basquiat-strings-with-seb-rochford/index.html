Basquiat Strings with Seb Rochford

Basquiat Strings with Seb Rochford is a unique collaboration that brings together the worlds of jazz, classical, and contemporary music. The project is centered around Basquiat Strings, a string ensemble led by cellist and composer Ben Davis. The ensemble was formed with the intention of blending the rich textures and sophisticated arrangements of a string quartet with the improvisational spirit of jazz. Seb Rochford, a renowned drummer and composer known for his versatility and innovative approach, joined forces with Basquiat Strings to further enhance this hybrid sound. Seb Rochford's contributions add a distinctive rhythmic layer to the ensemble, drawing on his extensive background in jazz and experimental music. His ability to navigate complex time signatures and his creative use of percussion complement the lush string arrangements, resulting in a dynamic and exploratory musical experience. The collaboration has been acclaimed for its inventive compositions and compelling live performances. Their eponymous debut album, released in 2007, earned a Mercury Prize nomination, highlighting its unique blend of genres and the masterful musicianship of the ensemble. Basquiat Strings with Seb Rochford continues to intrigue audiences with their boundary-pushing soundscapes and genre-defying artistry.
