Barry Can't Swim

Barry Can't Swim is the artistic moniker of Edinburgh-born DJ and music producer Joshua Mainnie. Known for his unique blend of house, jazz, and electronica, Barry Can't Swim has quickly carved out a niche in the vibrant UK electronic music scene. Mainnie's musical journey began in his teenage years when he explored various genres and honed his craft in production. Emerging onto the scene in the late 2010s, Barry Can't Swim captures listeners with his dynamic beats, soulful melodies, and intricate production techniques. His music often incorporates elements of world music, creating a rich tapestry that is both hypnotic and danceable. Releases like "Amor Fati" and "Jazz Hands" have garnered significant attention, showcasing his ability to seamlessly blend rhythmic complexity with melodic warmth. Drawing inspiration from diverse musical influences, Barry Can't Swim has established himself as a prominent figure in electronic music circles. His performances are noted for their energy and creativity, making him a sought-after artist for festivals and club nights. With each release, Barry Can't Swim continues to push the boundaries of electronic music while maintaining an accessibility that appeals to a broad audience.
