Novelist

Novelist, born Kwadwo Quentin Kankam on January 20, 1997, is a British grime artist renowned for his contributions to the UK music scene. Raised in Lewisham, South London, Novelist began his musical journey at a young age, quickly drawing attention with his distinctive style and formidable lyrical prowess. He emerged as a significant figure in the grime genre during the early 2010s, gaining recognition as part of the collective The Square. Novelist's breakout moment came with the release of singles like "Take Time" in 2014, a collaboration with fellow producer Mumdance, which showcased his ability to blend traditional grime elements with innovative sounds. His debut album, "Novelist Guy," released in 2018, further solidified his reputation as a leading voice in grime. The album earned critical acclaim for its introspective lyrics and innovative production. Throughout his career, Novelist has been celebrated for his DIY ethic, often producing and releasing music independently. He is also known for his political engagement, using his platform to address social issues. With a unique voice and an unwavering commitment to his craft, Novelist continues to push the boundaries of grime music.
