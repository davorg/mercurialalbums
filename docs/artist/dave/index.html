Dave

Dave, whose real name is David Orobosa Omoregie, is a highly acclaimed British rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer born on June 5, 1998, in Brixton, South London. He rose to prominence with his introspective lyricism and socially conscious themes, capturing the essence of modern British life. Dave first gained attention in the mid-2010s with his mixtapes and singles, most notably "Thiago Silva," a collaboration with fellow UK rapper AJ Tracey. His debut album, "Psychodrama" (2019), received critical acclaim for its narrative brilliance and depth, exploring themes of mental health, identity, and systemic racism. It won the prestigious Mercury Prize and the Brit Award for Album of the Year. Known for his poignant storytelling, Dave's music often reflects his Nigerian heritage and South London upbringing. In addition to his solo work, Dave has collaborated with various artists, expanding his influence beyond the UK grime scene. With his sharp lyricism and insightful commentary, Dave has become a voice of his generation, using his platform to address important social issues while captivating audiences worldwide.
