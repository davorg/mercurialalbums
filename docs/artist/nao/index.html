Nao

Nao, born Neo Jessica Joshua on December 20, 1987, in Nottingham, England, is a British singer, songwriter, and record producer known for her distinctive blend of neo-soul, funk, R&B, and electronic music. Growing up in East London, she was exposed to a rich tapestry of musical influences and began her formal music education at the Guildhall School of Music & Drama. Nao first emerged on the music scene in 2014 with the release of her debut EP, "So Good," featuring the standout track "Back Porch." Her unique sound caught the attention of critics and fans alike, leading to her nomination for the BBC Sound of 2016 poll. Her debut album, "For All We Know," released in 2016, further established her as a prominent figure in the modern soul scene, with tracks like "Bad Blood" and "Girlfriend" showcasing her ability to blend genres seamlessly. In 2018, Nao released her critically acclaimed second album, "Saturn," exploring themes of transformation and self-reflection. Her soulful voice and innovative approach to music have earned her a dedicated fan base and recognition, including a Grammy nomination for Best Urban Contemporary Album. Nao continues to influence the music landscape with her evocative and dynamic artistry.
