Fred Again

Fred Again, born Frederick John Philip Gibson on July 19, 1993, in London, England, is a British musician, songwriter, and record producer renowned for his transformative approach to music. Known professionally as Fred Again, he came to prominence through his innovative fusion of electronic, pop, and ambient music. As a multi-instrumentalist, Fred developed a keen ear for music from an early age, studying at the prestigious Westminster School before embarking on a career in music production. Fred Again began his career working alongside acclaimed producer Brian Eno, who became a mentor and collaborator. His unique blending of everyday sounds, emotional narratives, and electronic beats define his distinct style. He's worked with a diverse array of artists, including Ed Sheeran, Stormzy, and FKA Twigs, contributing to their hits and further cementing his industry reputation.
