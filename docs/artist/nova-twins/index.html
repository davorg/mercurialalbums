Nova Twins

Nova Twins are a dynamic duo hailing from London, known for their high-octane blend of punk, rock, and electronic elements. Formed in 2014, the band consists of vocalist and guitarist Amy Love and bassist Georgia South. Both members bring a unique energy and style, which has been pivotal in defining Nova Twins' distinctive sound. Their music is characterized by powerful guitar riffs, gritty basslines, and energetic vocals, all laced with a rebellious spirit that challenges genre boundaries. Nova Twins quickly rose to prominence with their debut EP, showcasing their ability to seamlessly fuse different musical influences, from grime to punk rock. The band's dedication to authenticity and their DIY approach to music and image has garnered them a dedicated fanbase. They gained further acclaim with their debut album, "Who Are the Girls?" released in 2020, which amplified their mission to create space for women of color in rock music. Recognized not just for their music but also for their outspoken views on societal issues, Nova Twins continue to be a force for change within the music industry, inspiring a new generation of artists and fans alike with their fearless innovation and unapologetic expression.
