Shygirl

Shygirl, born Blane Muise in South London, is a multifaceted artist known for her innovative approach to music and distinct aesthetic. Emerging from the vibrant London club scene, she made her mark as a singer, rapper, and DJ. Her sound is a bold fusion of genres, incorporating elements of electronic, pop, and hip-hop, often characterized by edgy production and provocative lyrics. Shygirl co-founded the creative collective and record label NUXXE, alongside other avant-garde artists like SOPHIE and Sega Bodega, further cementing her place within the experimental music sphere. Her debut EP, "Cruel Practice," released in 2018, quickly garnered attention for its boundary-pushing sound and raw energy. This project was followed by the critically acclaimed EP "Alias" in 2020, featuring standout tracks such as "SLIME" and "FREAK." Known for her stylistic boldness, Shygirl also frequently collaborates with other boundary-pushing artists and designers, enhancing her reputation as a cutting-edge cultural figure. Her work continues to resonate both in the underground scene and increasingly within the mainstream, signaling her as a transformative force in contemporary music.
