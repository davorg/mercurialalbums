Blossoms

Blossoms is an English indie pop band formed in Stockport, Greater Manchester, in 2013. The group consists of five members: Tom Ogden (lead vocals, guitar), Charlie Salt (bass, backing vocals), Josh Dewhurst (lead guitar, percussion), Joe Donovan (drums), and Myles Kellock (keyboards, synthesizers, backing vocals). Blossoms first gained attention with their self-released singles "Blow" and "Cut Me and I'll Bleed" in 2014, showcasing a blend of synth-driven melodies and catchy pop hooks. In August 2016, Blossoms released their eponymous debut album, "Blossoms," which quickly climbed to the top of the UK Albums Chart. The album featured hit singles such as "Charlemagne" and "Honey Sweet," solidifying their reputation as one of the promising new acts in the British indie scene. Their music draws influences from a variety of genres, including new wave, pop, and rock, often characterized by its infectious energy and nostalgic undertones. Blossoms continued their success with subsequent albums, "Cool Like You" (2018) and "Foolish Loving Spaces" (2020), each expanding their sound while retaining their distinctive charm. With their engaging live performances and vibrant sound, Blossoms have become a staple of modern British pop music.
