Wolf Alice

Wolf Alice is a British alternative rock band formed in 2010 in London, England. Initially a folk solo project by singer and guitarist Ellie Rowsell, the band expanded into a four-piece with the addition of guitarist Joff Oddie, drummer Joel Amey, and bassist Theo Ellis. Known for their eclectic sound that blends elements of grunge, folk, and electronic music, Wolf Alice has garnered critical acclaim and a dedicated fanbase. Their breakthrough came with the release of their debut album, *My Love Is Cool*, in 2015, which received widespread praise and earned them a Grammy nomination. The album's lead single, "Moaning Lisa Smile," gained significant attention, helping to establish their presence in the alternative music scene. The band's second album, *Visions of a Life*, released in 2017, won the prestigious Mercury Prize, further solidifying their status as one of the UK's leading contemporary rock bands. With dynamic live performances and a knack for genre-blending, Wolf Alice continues to evolve, releasing their third album, *Blue Weekend*, in 2021, to critical acclaim. Their ability to capture raw emotion and authenticity in their music remains a hallmark of their enduring appeal.
