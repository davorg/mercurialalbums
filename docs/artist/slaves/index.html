Slaves

Slaves is a punk rock duo hailing from Kent, England, formed in 2012. The band consists of Laurie Vincent on guitar, bass, and vocals, and Isaac Holman on drums and vocals. Known for their raw, energetic sound and satirical lyrics, Slaves quickly garnered attention with their aggressive, stripped-back approach to punk music. Their debut album, *Are You Satisfied?*, released in 2015, was met with critical acclaim and showcased their ability to blend humor and social commentary within their songs. The album earned a nomination for the Mercury Prize, propelling the duo onto the UK music scene and solidifying their reputation for infectious live performances. Slaves continued to build on their success with subsequent releases like *Take Control* (2016) and *Acts of Fear and Love* (2018), further developing their distinctive sound. Across their discography, they have addressed themes such as consumerism, societal pressures, and personal struggles, often employing a mix of irony and directness. With a dedicated fan base and a reputation for electrifying shows, Slaves has become a staple in the UK punk scene, continually pushing boundaries while maintaining their rebellious spirit.
