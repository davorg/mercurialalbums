Yard Act

Yard Act is a British rock band formed in 2019 in Leeds, England. The band consists of vocalist James Smith, bassist Ryan Needham, guitarist Sam Shipstone, and drummer Jay Russell. They quickly gained attention for their witty, socially observant lyrics and genre-spanning sound, which blends post-punk, indie rock, and a touch of funk. Yard Act first garnered widespread attention with their debut EP, "Dark Days," released in 2020, which showcased their knack for storytelling and sharp commentary on contemporary life. Their music often addresses themes such as capitalism, societal inequality, and everyday absurdities, wrapped in catchy hooks and driving rhythms. The band's debut album, "The Overload," was released in January 2022 to critical acclaim, earning praise for its clever lyricism and energetic performances. Yard Act's unique sound and engaging live shows have positioned them as one of the most exciting new acts to emerge from the UK music scene in recent years. With their insightful and often humorous approach to songwriting, Yard Act continues to build a dedicated fanbase both at home and internationally.
