corto.alto

Corto.alto is a dynamic musical project founded by Scottish multi-instrumentalist, composer, and producer Liam Shortall. Emerging from Glasgow’s vibrant jazz scene, corto.alto has quickly gained a reputation for its innovative fusion of jazz, hip-hop, electronic, and funk influences. Initially forming as a collaborative project, Shortall enlisted a rotating lineup of talented musicians, bringing together diverse sounds and styles. Since its inception, corto.alto has gained acclaim for its engaging live performances and unique approach to the jazz genre, characterized by improvisational flair and an eclectic blend of musical genres. The collective’s music often incorporates modern production techniques, resulting in fresh and genre-defying tracks that resonate with a wide array of listeners. Corto.alto has released a series of well-received EPs and singles, each showcasing the ensemble’s ability to seamlessly meld traditional jazz elements with contemporary sounds. Their work not only highlights the individual virtuosity of its members but also emphasizes collaborative synergy. As they continue to evolve, corto.alto remains a compelling force in the modern jazz landscape, bridging the gap between classic and contemporary music forms.
