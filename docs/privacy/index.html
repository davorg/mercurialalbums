Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how this website collects and uses information.

Cookies

This website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and to understand how the site is used.

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings.

Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics (GA4), a web analytics service provided by Google, to help understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics uses cookies and collects information such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and general geographic location (e.g. country or city level).

This information is used only to understand how the website is used and to improve the site. The information is collected in an anonymised form and is not used to identify individual users.

You can find out more about how Google uses data here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to personalise advertising. This is known as interest-based advertising.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting: https://adssettings.google.com/

For more information about how Google uses data in advertising products, see: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
